About Us

Handcrafted home décor made from natural vines, leaves and Natural materials — proudly crafted by rural women artisans.

At EcoLiana Handicrafts, we celebrate the harmony between nature and artistry. Our mission is to bring the serenity and beauty of nature into your home through the skilled craftsmanship of rural women artisans. Each creation is lovingly handcrafted from natural vines, leaves, and other eco-friendly materials — reflecting the essence of sustainable living and timeless design.

We believe that creativity should be as personal as it is purposeful. That’s why we offer you the freedom to customize every detail — from design and size to the choice of natural materials — making each piece truly one of a kind.

Every purchase you make goes beyond aesthetics — it becomes part of a meaningful journey. A share of each sale directly supports underprivileged rural women, helping them achieve financial independence and social empowerment. Together, we’re not just crafting décor; we’re nurturing a connection between nature, creativity, and community.
