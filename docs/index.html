Inspired by Nature...

Do you love nature and seek to bring its serenity into your home? At EcoLiana Handicrafts, we transform this passion into beautifully crafted, eco-friendly home décor. Each piece is handmade by skilled rural women using natural green leaves, fibers, cane, bamboo, jute and other sustainable materials. Our collection includes baskets, mats, decorative items, and more — all designed to add a touch of rustic charm and warmth to your living space. By choosing our handcrafted products, you support sustainable practices, empower rural artisans, and bring authentic, nature-inspired décor to your home.
